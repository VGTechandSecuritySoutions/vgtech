Seaing Reality Adventures

Full Brand Identity & Marketing Setup

A complete branding and launch project for a local tour company in Caye Caulker, Belize.

Project Story

Seaing Reality Adventures is a snorkeling, fishing, and private tour company focused on delivering authentic and adventurous island experiences. The goal was to create a strong, professional identity that reflects both the local culture and the excitement of marine tours.

The client originally had a low-quality logo made from layered images. This project involved completely redesigning the brand into a clean, meaningful, and scalable identity, along with creating marketing materials and setting up their online presence.

Project Breakdown

Logo Design

A completely redesigned logo featuring a circular ocean-inspired layout, incorporating marine life, snorkeling elements, and a fishing boat silhouette. The Belize map was creatively integrated into the typography to represent location and identity.

Business Card Design

Designed a clean and functional business card including WhatsApp contact, location, and Facebook presence, making it easy for customers to connect and book tours.

Flyer Design

Created promotional flyers to be distributed at hotels, highlighting available tours and services. The design focused on attracting tourists and encouraging referrals from hotel staff.

Facebook Page Setup

Complete setup including profile and cover design, business description, and initial content, giving the company a professional and trustworthy online presence.

Video Editing

Edited promotional video content from raw footage provided by the client, helping showcase real tour experiences and attract potential customers online.

Results & Impact

The new branding and marketing materials significantly improved the company's professional image, helping build trust with potential customers. The business began receiving inquiries and gained visibility both through physical promotion and online presence.
