Island Boy Ice Factory

Brand Identity & Promotional Materials

Designed in collaboration with Visual Graphics, the creative partner of VG Tech.

Project Story

Island Boy Ice Factory, a wholesale ice business in Caye Caulker, Belize, wanted a brand that would reflect both its heritage and professionalism. The client requested a logo featuring a lobster, referencing the owner’s fishing and lobster business, as well as an ice box. Visual Graphics took this direction and crafted a unique, eye-catching identity. In addition to the logo, business cards, flyers, and a price list were created to provide a consistent and professional visual presence.

Since launch, the branding has helped Island Boy Ice Factory stand out and be widely recognized across the island, elevating the business’s image and customer trust.
