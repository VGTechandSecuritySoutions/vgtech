01 · The Brief

Heritage on the rocks.

Island Boy Ice Factory is a wholesale & retail ice business in Caye Caulker — a sandy strip of Belize where the lobster traps outnumber the lampposts. The owner came to us with one ask: a brand that respected the work behind it, and the island it came from.

No clip-art penguins. No corporate snowflakes. Something that could sit on a 40lb ice bag headed to a restaurant kitchen, on a delivery cart, and on the side of a cooler at a roadside bar — and feel equally at home in every one of those places.

"Make it look like us. Not like every other ice company on Google."

— The brief, summarized

02 · Brand DNA

Three pillars carved
from the same block.

Every visual decision came back to these. If a mark didn't pass all three, it didn't ship.

01

Rooted

The lobster — a daily reality on Caye Caulker — anchors the mark in place. It's not decoration, it's a passport.

02

Reliable

The ice box silhouette is steady, square, dependable. Restaurants run on the assumption their ice will arrive fresh and on time.

03

Recognizable

Bold typography and high-contrast color make the brand legible across a bag of ice in a freezer or on the back of a delivery cart.

03 · The Mark

A logo that reads
at fifty feet
and on a sticker.

Two icons, one story. The ice box says the job. The lobster says the island. The serif and the slab together carry weight — like a hand-painted sign that's been there longer than you have.

  • Ice box. Speaks to the trade — wholesale, retail, cold chain, dependable.
  • Lobster. Owner's heritage. A nod to Caye Caulker's fishing community.
  • Type lockup. Bold display + condensed support — designed to scale.

05 · Process

From a frozen brief
to a brand that ships.

The Visual Graphics process — same four steps for every project, whether it's an ice factory or a fashion boutique.

  1. 01

    Discover

    Conversations with the owner. Walks past the existing setup. Photos of the ice bags, the gulf cart, the freezer.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Three directions: literal (the box), heritage (the lobster), and a hybrid lock-up combining both stories.

  3. 03

    Refine

    We tuned proportions, tested it small (sticker), large (signage), and on a real ice bag mockup.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Final files in every format the business needs — SVG, PNG, PDF, mono versions, social cuts.
