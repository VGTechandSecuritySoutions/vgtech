M&N Guest House

Logo Vectorization & Digital Recreation

This project involved digitally recreating an existing logo that was originally hand-painted on a physical sign. The goal was to preserve its unique identity while transforming it into a clean, scalable digital format.

Since the original design did not have accessible digital assets, we carefully analyzed the lettering and visual elements, identified matching fonts, and manually traced components to achieve a highly accurate vector version.

The final result maintains the authenticity of the original artwork while allowing the client to use the logo across modern platforms, including social media, print materials, and web.
