M&N Guest House

Logo Vectorization & Brand Restoration

This project focused on digitally recreating and restoring the existing logo of M&N Guest House, which was originally hand-painted on a physical sign and had begun to fade over time.

To preserve its recognizable identity, we carefully analyzed the original artwork, matched the closest possible fonts, and manually traced each element to produce a clean and accurate vector version. During this process, we also enhanced the color tones—maintaining the original palette while improving vibrancy and consistency for digital use.

The final logo retains the authentic look of the original design while being fully optimized for modern applications, ensuring it can be used across web, print, and promotional materials without quality loss.

Deliverables

• Scalable SVG file for high-quality use at any size
• PNG files for web and digital platforms
• Optimized layout for both print and online use

Additional Work

In addition to the logo restoration, we also designed and developed the official website for M&N Guest House through VG Tech & Security Solutions, creating a complete digital presence for the business.
