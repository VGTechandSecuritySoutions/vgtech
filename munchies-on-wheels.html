Munchies on Wheels

Logo, Trailer Wrap & Branding for a Local Food Trailer

Complete branding solution for the first fully branded and designed food trailer on Caye Caulker, creating a strong and professional visual identity.

Project Story

Munchies on Wheels is the rebranded version of a local establishment formerly known as After Hour Munchies, which catered to late-night club-goers. Now operating from a fully equipped mobile trailer, the business wanted to stand out and establish a professional presence.

The goal was to create a cohesive brand identity that could be applied across the trailer, menu, and promotional materials, making it instantly recognizable and helping the business gain attention in a competitive market.

Design Approach

Logo Design

The logo was created to capture the fun, energetic, and approachable nature of the business while remaining professional. It needed to be flexible for various applications, including print and digital platforms.

Trailer Wrap & Menu Design

We designed a full trailer wrap that not only stands out visually but also reinforces the brand's identity. The menu was incorporated directly onto the trailer's service door, ensuring visibility and readability for customers.

These design elements create a unified and professional look, making Munchies on Wheels the first food trailer on Caye Caulker with a fully branded and cohesive identity.

Real Business Impact

Since the rebrand, Munchies on Wheels has become the most recognized trailer of its kind on the island. The new professional identity attracts more attention and sets the business apart from competitors, demonstrating the value of strong visual branding.
