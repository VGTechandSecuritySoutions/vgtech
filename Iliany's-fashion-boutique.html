Iliany's Fashion Boutique

Logo Design for a Growing Boutique & Lifestyle Store

Branding created to represent a versatile boutique in San Narciso, Corozal, Belize, offering clothing, home products, and more.

Project Story

Iliany's Fashion Boutique started as a small clothing and accessories shop and has expanded to offer a wide range of products imported directly from the United States, including clothes, kitchen appliances, electronics, vitamins, toys, and more.

The boutique operates by sourcing products via vehicles packed in the US and transported through Mexico to Belize, giving customers access to a variety of high-quality goods at competitive prices.

Design Approach

Logo Design

The logo features a hanger to represent fashion and within it, a cup symbolizing home and kitchen products. This design creates a cohesive identity that reflects the boutique’s expansion from clothing to a variety of lifestyle products.

The goal was to make the boutique appear professional, trustworthy, and recognizable, helping Iliany establish a strong presence in Corozal’s market.

Real Business Impact

The new logo helped Iliany's Fashion Boutique look more legitimate and professional, strengthening customer trust. It communicates the business’s growth and versatility, showing that it’s more than just a clothing store, but a complete lifestyle and home goods shop.
