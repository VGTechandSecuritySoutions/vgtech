Creative Haven

Logo Design for a Custom Printing & Engraving Business

A clean and professional brand identity created for a growing small business specializing in customized products.

Project Story

Creative Haven is a small but fast-growing business offering custom printing and engraving services, including personalized cups, bags, keychains, party items, and more.

With a high demand for custom orders and limited time for marketing, the goal was to create a strong, professional logo that could represent the business consistently across all products.

Design Approach

Logo Design

The logo was designed from scratch with a focus on simplicity, clarity, and professionalism. The goal was to create a flexible identity that works across a wide range of custom products.

Since the business produces a variety of items—from engraved products to printed packaging—the design needed to remain clean and adaptable for different sizes, surfaces, and materials.

The final result is a modern and recognizable brand mark that supports the business as it continues to grow.

Real Business Impact

Even without heavy marketing, Creative Haven continues to receive a steady flow of orders, showing strong demand for their services. The new logo provides a consistent and professional identity that supports customer trust and brand recognition.
