E-Z Boy Tours

Full Digital Transformation & Branding Support

A complete upgrade of digital presence, booking systems, branding assets, and business infrastructure for a growing tour company.

Project Story

The project began when E-Z Boy Tours experienced a compromised email account, affecting communication and bookings.

From this starting point, the project evolved into a complete digital transformation, improving their website, branding, booking process, and overall business operations.

Services & Solutions

Website Optimization & SEO

The website was upgraded with custom code, meta keywords, and AI visibility configurations.

The business now ranks significantly higher in search results for marine tours.

Booking System Integration

A full booking and payment system was integrated using Tab.travel, making reservations easier for customers.

Email Security & Setup

The compromised email was secured and Google Workspace was implemented to improve reliability and professionalism.

Branding & Marketing

The logo was vectorized and marketing materials like flyers, vouchers, and QR stickers were created to improve engagement and bookings.

IT & Business Systems

Cameras, networking, and a full Linktree system were set up to act as a digital business hub for customers.

Real Business Impact

The business now receives more bookings, better reviews, and operates with a stronger and more professional digital presence.
